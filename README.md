## 👤 About Me

I'm a Renewable Energy Engineering student (Universidad Autónoma de Aguascalientes) and early-career researcher/developer working at the intersection of **computer vision, deep learning, and sustainable technology**. My work spans video-based fish identification, aquaculture-oriented morphological analysis, and Edge AI for environmental monitoring — built on neural-network workflows with **YOLO, Mask R-CNN, OpenCV**, and knowledge of **PyTorch/TensorFlow**.

I've carried this work internationally through research collaborations in the **Czech Republic** (VSB–Technical University of Ostrava, University of South Bohemia) and **Germany** (Technische Universität Chemnitz, IAESTE), alongside several years of remote freelance software delivery and STEAM/robotics education in Mexico.

- 🔬 Research interests: video understanding, computer vision in aquaculture, deep learning for environmental monitoring, AI for sustainable development
- 🚀 Founder of **AquaGuard** — AI + IoT water-monitoring venture (2nd place, UAA Entrepreneurial Bootcamp 2024)
- 🌍 International research experience across México, Czech Republic, and Germany
- 🗣️ Spanish (native) · English (full professional proficiency)

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🐟 [FISHDEX](https://github.com/DanielAguilarJ/FISHDEX) — Video-Based Individual Fish Identification
Research application built during the VSB–Ostrava Summer School: users capture short fish videos on mobile, and a server-side computer vision pipeline performs individual identification and returns the fish's identity.
**Stack:** Flutter (Dart) · Python AI server · OBB/ROI detection · custom species classifier

### 💧 [AquaGuard (FugasApp)](https://github.com/DanielAguilarJ/AQUAGUARD) — Edge AI Water-Leak Detection
Android app + IoT sensor system monitoring flow, pressure, and vibration in water pipelines. On-device ML predicts leaks up to **24 hours in advance**, with explainable AI (XAI) behind every alert. 🏆 2nd place, UAA Entrepreneurial Bootcamp 2024.
**Stack:** Jetpack Compose · Kotlin · TensorFlow Lite · Retrofit · Firebase Cloud Messaging · MVVM · JUnit/MockK

### 🤖 [Reclutify](https://github.com/DanielAguilarJ/reclutify) — AI Interview Platform
Recruitment SaaS platform that conducts automated voice/text interviews through an AI interviewer ("Zara") and generates objective, bias-audited performance evaluations.
**Stack:** Next.js 15 (App Router) · TypeScript · Supabase · Tailwind CSS v4 · OpenRouter AI · Cloudflare R2 · Vitest — *proprietary, © WorldBrain EdTech*

### 🌅 [ChronoGuard](https://github.com/DanielAguilarJ/Flux-windows) — Adaptive Monitor Color Temperature
Native Windows desktop app that gradually adjusts monitor color temperature based on the local solar position, reducing eye strain — with a Fluent Design / glassmorphism UI.
**Stack:** C# · .NET 8.0 · Windows API · WPF/WinForms
